Operations & Review

    
Run funding intelligence jobs and review every proposed database change.

    


      

        
Follow-up Reminders

        
Check for reports at 30/60/90-day milestones with no tracking, and email reminders. Runs daily at 07:00 UTC.

        
        

      


      

        
Weekly Monitor

        
Scrape InvestNI, DAERA, DfC and CFNI for fund changes. Runs Mondays at 08:00 UTC.

        
        

      


      

        
Funding Radar

        
Annotate funds with seasonal opening patterns. Runs on the 1st of each month at 09:00 UTC.

        
        

      


      

        
AI Discovery

        
Search the wider NI funding landscape for new schemes, validate + enrich each, and drop candidates into the review queue above. Costs a few £ per run.

        
        

      


      

        
Re-verify Existing Funds

        
Re-read the source page of every active fund and propose grounded corrections (wrong grant sizes, passed deadlines, fillable blanks) into the “Proposed updates” queue above. Costs a few £; leave the box blank to do all, or set a limit.

        
        
        

      


    


    

      

        
Discovered funds — pending review

Source confidence = how sure the engine is it read the funder's page correctly — it is not a client match score.
